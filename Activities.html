Activities you can do in the park

Picnic Bliss:

Gather loved ones for a delightful picnic, sharing snacks and laughter amidst the park's serene beauty.

Sports Extravaganza:

Engage in lively outdoor sports such as frisbee, badminton, or volleyball for active and social recreation.

Nature Strolls:

Embark on a scenic nature walk or hike, exploring the park's trails and appreciating its diverse flora and fauna.

Read and Relax:

Unwind with a good book or simply bask in the tranquility on a cozy blanket, surrounded by nature's embrace.

Capture the Moment:

Embrace your inner photographer, capturing scenic moments and documenting the park's vibrant atmosphere.
